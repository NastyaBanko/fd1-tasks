Beginning of life with art
I started drawing when I was a child. My parents influenced me. Every time i saw father's pictures, i tried to solve them, find their true meanings.
The best topic for my works is people because they are very difficult to understand. People can inspire you to do something and can destroy all your wishes.
I don't understand society, who loves nature more than people. For me, nature is beautiful, but monotonous. But it's hard to find facsinating person too.
